About

Why Deli Express?

Lunch-hour patterns show students either forgoing meals or arriving simultaneously at the deli, resulting in prolonged queues. Deli Express uses scheduled pre-ordering to distribute demand and accelerate pickup.

  • Timed pickup windows
    5-minute slots flatten peak times.
  • Express pre-order lane
    Orders are staged before you arrive.
  • Live ETA & queue preview
    Choose the best timeslot and avoid the rush.
  • Better choices, faster
    Nutrition & allergen tags at a glance.
